About Me.

I am Yegor, 22 years old, from Kazakhstan. In May 2024, I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at the University of British Columbia with Distinction. I worked as a teaching assistant for a machine learning course at UBC during the summer term of 2023.

International living experiences, particularly in Kazakhstan, the UAE, Turkey, and Canada, have shaped my broader perspective and adaptability to new environments, which I value greatly.

I'm fluent in both English and Russian, which have been useful in connecting with diverse people and cultures along the way.

Outside of academics, sports have played a significant role in my life. I have 2 years of both: soccer and boxing; together with 4 years total of gym and/or calisthenics. I’ve also played the flute and the alto saxophone.

I truly value building meaningful connections, as friendships have been an important part of my life, especially throughout my time in different countries.
